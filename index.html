Sound of Metal

"Sound of Metal" is a poignant drama following the life of a heavy metal drummer, Ruben, whose world is shattered when he begins to lose his hearing. The film explores themes of identity, acceptance, and the transformative power of resilience. Riz Ahmed delivers a powerful performance as Ruben, capturing the emotional struggles of adapting to a silent world. With its immersive sound design and compelling narrative, "Sound of Metal" offers an intimate and thought-provoking exploration of the human experience in the face of profound change. Read More here
